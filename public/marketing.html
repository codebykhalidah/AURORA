The Ship

A private yacht with the soul of a grand voyage.

Aurora is engineered for rare access: shallow-draft approach, silent electric harbor maneuvering, and a hospitality ratio that feels closer to a private residence than a cruise ship.

Designed for silence

Hybrid propulsion and acoustic insulation keep the ship nearly silent at anchor, so mornings sound like water, wind, and nothing else.

Fifty guests maximum

Every voyage is capped at 50 guests and served by 45 crew, enabling a level of recognition and discretion rarely possible at sea.
